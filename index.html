Academic Work · UCLA Anderson Marketing Management

Nike Studios: Market Entry Strategy

🔍 3 C's Analysis

  • Company: Brand trust, rotating training formats, NTC app ecosystem
  • Competition: Equinox, Barry's, F45, Orangetheory — mapped by location
  • Customer: UWS (affluent, family-driven) vs. LES (young creatives, price-sensitive)

🎯 STP Framework

  • Segments: Professionals, young creatives, students — by geography and behavior
  • Target: UWS professionals (premium LTV) + LES creatives (trend influence)
  • Position: Premium community fitness for UWS; authentic, flexible for LES

📦 4 P's Strategy

  • Product: Rotating formats + athlete-led programs + wearable integration
  • Place: UWS — 83rd & Columbus, Central Park adjacency, 6 subway lines
  • Promotion: Premium membership + athlete brand moments + word-of-mouth
The discipline behind this: Good segmentation goes beyond demographics — it's about understanding what actually drives a decision. Whether the buyer is a student choosing a university or a contractor evaluating software, the same question applies: what does this person actually care about, and are we leading with that? That's the lens I bring to every brief.
